The goal of this project is to rehaul the current Budget Generator that the AgEcon department currently uses. Their current software was last updated in 2008 and they have no way to edit the code, so if this Budget Generator breaks they have no way of fixing it. We plan to use a variety of resources to make this project perform as best as possible for the user. We will begin with a user-friendly UI/UX design and implement this design with Html5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and Javascript.
