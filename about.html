about us

------------------


vision

The current agecon's budget calculator software from the pre 2000s is extremely outdated. This year, the agecon department wishes to revamp that software into a modern web application that users can access at home. The new Agricultural Economics Budget Generator is a tool for farmers to utilize when planning their annual farming budget. This product will be free for public use and managed by Mississippi State University's Agricultural Economic department.

goals

This web application will serve as a tool to reduce yearly cost, in terms of time and money. The new software will lead to increased reliability of budget generation, decreased complexity of operation, and increased efficiency of maintenance. This budget generator being developed should allow farmers to create, save, and edit budget plans based on information entered by the site managers from Mississippi State University.
